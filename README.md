☕ Coffee Shop Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard
📊 Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, and customer purchasing behavior in a coffee retail business. The dashboard provides a clear and visually engaging way to explore key business metrics and uncover actionable insights that support data-driven decision making.

This project focuses on transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights using Excel analytics techniques such as Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, slicers, and KPI indicators. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor performance across different product categories, locations, and time periods.

🎯 Key Objectives

Monitor overall sales and revenue performance

Identify top-performing products

Analyze customer purchase patterns by time and day

Compare sales distribution across store locations

Provide an interactive filtering experience using category slicers

📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights several important KPIs including:

Total Revenue: $698,812

Products Sold: 214,470

Total Orders: 149,116

Number of Products: 80

Average Unit Price: $3.4

These metrics provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

📈 Dashboard Insights
Revenue Trend

The Revenue by Month chart shows a steady growth from January to June, indicating increasing customer demand and improved sales performance over time.

Sales by Time of Day

Customer purchasing behavior is heavily concentrated in the morning (55%), followed by afternoon sales (36%), while night sales contribute only 9%.

Top Performing Products

The Top Products analysis identifies items such as Dark Chocolate, Cappuccino, and Latte as the leading revenue generators.

Sales by Day

The Sales by Day visualization shows that Friday and Monday generate the highest sales, while Saturday records comparatively lower sales.

Sales by Location

Sales distribution across Hell's Kitchen, Lower Manhattan, and Astoria remains relatively balanced, with each location contributing roughly one-third of total sales.
